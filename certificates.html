Certifications

Some of my important wins in competitions held in College.

Poster Presentation by CSI

Won first prize in the poster presentation competition held by the Computer Society of India. My topic was "Smart Quill," where I discussed its technical features, pros and cons, and potential future improvements.

Code Golf

Secured third place in the Code Golf competition, where we solved basic LeetCode problems. Despite technical difficulties with the website at the start, I persevered and managed to finish strongly, even though I initially lost time rewriting my code.

Battle of Opinions

Achieved second place in the seminar competition "Battle of Opinions" during my fifth semester. The competition comprised two rounds: discussing whether technology is essential or a distraction, and whether AI is causing job losses. I took a contrarian stance, arguing that AI is not taking over jobs, and found the event both challenging and rewarding.

War of Worlds

Won the seminar competition on whether AI is a threat to future engineers and scholars. I presented my honest views, quoting Oppenheimer and Feynman, and emerged victorious in one of the most competitive and rewarding seminars I've participated in.
